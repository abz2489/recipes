Chicken, Leek & Tarragon Pie

Description

Indulge in a savory delight with our chicken, leek, and tarragon pie. Tender chicken chunks meld seamlessly with the sweet, delicate leeks, all enveloped in a creamy tarragon-infused sauce. Encased in a golden, flaky pastry crust, each bite offers a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that promise pure comfort and satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Leeks
  • 1 Block Shortcrust Pastry
  • 125ml White Wine
  • 300ml Chicken Stock
  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Plain Flour
  • 1tbsp Tarragon
