Chicken, Leek & Tarragon Pie

Description

Indulge in a savory delight with our chicken, leek, and tarragon pie. Tender chicken chunks meld seamlessly with the sweet, delicate leeks, all enveloped in a creamy tarragon-infused sauce. Encased in a golden, flaky pastry crust, each bite offers a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that promise pure comfort and satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Leeks
  • 1 Block Shortcrust Pastry
  • 125ml White Wine
  • 300ml Chicken Stock
  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Plain Flour
  • 1tbsp Tarragon

Instructions

  1. Let the chicken come to room temperature before seasoning with salt and pepper.
  2. Pre heat the oven to 180 degrees. Heaat a frying pan over high heat. Seal the chicken for a few minutes on both sides.
  3. Transfer seared chicken to an oven safe tray and bake for 15 minutes.
  4. Melt 25g butter over medium head. Chop leeks and add to the melted butter, cooking until soft.
  5. When the leeks are soft add the remaining butter. When the butter is melted add the flour and mix together thoroughly
  6. Add the white wine gradually and mix continuously. Add the chicken stock gradually followed by 100ml milk
  7. Remove chicken from oven and leave to rest for 5-10 minutes. Once rested, shred the chicken with two forks.
  8. Add the shredded chicken to the leeks and season with salt and pepper. Chop tarragon and add to taste
  9. Roll out pastry, line and fill pastry dish
  10. Place pie in the oven at 180 degrees for 25-30 minutes
