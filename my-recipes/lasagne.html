Lasagne

Description

Savor the ultimate comfort food with our classic lasagne. Layers of perfectly cooked pasta cradle a rich, meaty Bolognese sauce, intertwined with velvety b√©chamel and melted mozzarella. Topped with a golden, bubbling cheese crust, each forkful delivers a harmonious blend of savory, creamy, and cheesy goodness that is utterly irresistible.

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Celery
  • 1 Large Carrot
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 125ml Red Wine
  • 1tpsb Tomato Puree
  • 400g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 Beef Stock Cube
  • 1tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 Sqaures Dark Chocolate
  • Lasagne sheets
  • 25g Butter
  • 25g Plain Flour
  • 25g Parmesan
  • 200ml Milk
  • 1tsp Dijon Mustard
  • Nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Brown mince, when browned set aside for later
  2. Finely dice the onion, carrot, celery and garlic.
  3. Add onion, carrot and celery to the frying pan over medium heat until softened.
  4. Pour in the red wine and allow to cook down before mixing through the vegetables.
  5. Add the tomato puree, mix in throughly with the red wine vegetables. Cook off stirring occasionally until the tomato puree takes a darker colour.
  6. Pour in the two tins of tomatoes and add the mince back to the pan.
  7. Add the beef stock cube and the Worcestershire sauce and mix thoroughly
  8. Season with salt and pepper before adding the chocolate. Stir through until melted, this is your bolognese sauce.
  9. Melt the butter over a low heat
  10. When the butter is melted add your flour and mix thoroughly to form a dough like texture. This is the roux, allow the roux to cook a little over a the low heat stirring often to avoid burning
  11. Gradually add the milk a tablspoon at a time and stir continuously. When the roux starts to thin you cand pour the milk more generously
  12. After all milk has ben added, turn up the heat and cook out the bechamel for 2-3 minutes
  13. Lower the heat, the sauce should be thicker. Now add in the dijon mustard and parmesan
  14. Finally grate in some nutmeg and season generously with salt and pepper to taste
  15. Layer the lasagne in an oven proof dish starting with bolognese topped with a spoon full of bechamel. Place the pasta sheets on top with another spoon full of bechamel. Repeat until you run out of mix or room in the dish.
  16. Use the rest of the bechamel to cover the top layer of lasagne, add grated mature cheddar and mozzarrella. Top with parmesan and bake at 180 degrees for 30-40 minutes.
